Music Mania

Get Peared

ReLit

ReLit is designed to teach reading comprehension and literacy. Through this software, students read stories at the appropriate reading level and answer various kinds of comprehension questions as they go through the story.

The King of Atlantis

Players move around the board, investigate various items, steal from or fight with other players and interact with the NPCs to achieve their goals within the game. They have to make decisions based on their current stats, money, items, and unachieved goals to get the most points before one of the players decides to end the game.
